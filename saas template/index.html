How it works

Up and running
in under 5 minutes

1

Create your workspace

Sign up, name your workspace, and invite your team. Flowdesk auto-creates a starter project so you're not staring at a blank screen.

2

Add your projects & tasks

Import from Trello, Asana, or start fresh. Use templates for sprints, product launches, or client work — pre-loaded with best practices.

3

Connect your tools

Link Slack for notifications, GitHub for PR tracking, and your calendar for deadline sync. Everything in one feed, zero fragmentation.

4

Ship and measure

Watch your team move. Use reports and dashboards to catch blockers early, celebrate wins, and keep every stakeholder informed.
